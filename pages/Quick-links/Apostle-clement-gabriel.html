Apostle Clement Gabriel

Early Life and Background

Apostle Clement Gabriel was born on the 5th of July in Agege, Lagos State, Nigeria, into the family of Owholeyi. Despite growing up in a very poor family, Apostle Clement exhibited an extraordinary hunger for the things of the Spirit from a very young age. Determined to rise above the limitations of his bloodline, he earnestly sought after God and aspired to live a life that glorified His name.

Education and Spiritual Awakening

He completed his secondary education at KEKE Secondary School and pursued higher education at the prestigious University of Ibadan, where he studied Theatre Arts. During his university years, Apostle Clement's spiritual fervor intensified. His insatiable desire to know God and experience the realities of the Spirit led him to spearhead a significant spiritual revival on campus. This movement brought many to Christ, facilitated profound encounters with the Holy Spirit, and manifested visible and tangible signs of God's power.

Leadership and Ministry

Even after his university days, Apostle Clement's influence continued to grow. He was appointed the chairman of the Nigerian Christian Corpers’ Fellowship (NCCF) during his National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) tenure. In this role, he provided spiritual leadership, mentoring many young Christians, and further extending his impact on the lives of countless individuals.

Ministry and Ongoing Impact

Since then, Apostle Clement Gabriel has remained a steadfast blessing to lives both within Nigeria and beyond its borders. His ministry is marked by intense prayer, worship, and a deep commitment to the Word of God. Through his ministry, many have experienced healing, deliverance, and profound spiritual transformations.

Authorship

In addition to his pastoral duties, Apostle Clement is also an accomplished author. In 2018, he published his insightful book, "Secrets in the Secret Place", which has been a source of inspiration and spiritual enlightenment to many readers, encouraging them to deepen their personal relationship with God.

Biblical Inspiration

Apostle Clement's life and ministry echo the profound truths found in Scripture. His dedication to knowing God and helping others do the same can be encapsulated in the words of Jeremiah 29:13: "You will seek me and find me when you seek me with all your heart."

Just as Paul urged Timothy to be an example in speech, conduct, love, faith, and purity (1 Timothy 4:12), Apostle Clement's life stands as a testimony to these virtues. His unwavering faith and commitment to God's work inspire all who come into contact with his ministry.

Apostle Clement Gabriel's journey from a humble background to becoming a beacon of God's power and love is a testament to what God can do through a life wholly surrendered to Him. His story continues to inspire and draw many into a deeper relationship with God, making him a revered and beloved leader in the body of Christ.
