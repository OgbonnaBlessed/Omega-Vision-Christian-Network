Dr. Pastor Gabriel Priscillia

Early Life and Background

Born on the 24th of July, 1996, in Lagos, Nigeria, Dr. Pastor Gabriel Priscillia’s journey began in a household where faith was the cornerstone. As the daughter of dedicated pastors, her early life was immersed in the teachings of Christ and the values of service, compassion, and devotion. From a tender age, Priscillia exhibited a profound spiritual hunger, spending countless hours in prayer and Bible study, laying the groundwork for a life that would be devoted to God.

Education and Academic Excellence

Pastor Priscillia’s pursuit of excellence extended beyond the spiritual realm. She completed her secondary education at King's College, where she was known for her intellectual brilliance and unwavering discipline. Her academic journey reached a pinnacle at Macdonald University, where she graduated with a first-class degree in Optometry. This remarkable achievement was not just a testament to her intellect but also to her determination and hard work. Her success in academia was a reflection of her belief in Colossians 3:23, "Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, as working for the Lord, not for human masters."

Ministry Involvement

Stepping into her role as the wife of Apostle Clement Gabriel, the General Overseer, Dr. Pastor Priscillia embraced her calling with grace and fervor. She has become a pivotal figure in the ministry, especially known for her mentorship of young women. Priscillia’s guidance helps them navigate life’s challenges, grow in their faith, and prepare for godly marriages. Her influence extends far and wide, touching lives with her wisdom and nurturing spirit.

From her teenage years, Priscillia’s heart has been burdened with a passion for intercession. She has tirelessly prayed for souls to come to Christ and remain steadfast in their faith. This commitment to intercessory prayer is a cornerstone of her ministry, reflecting Jesus' own intercessory role as described in Hebrews 7:25, "Therefore he is able to save completely those who come to God through him, because he always lives to intercede for them."

Personal and Family Life

In her personal life, Dr. Pastor Priscillia is a loving wife and mother. Her marriage to Apostle Clement Gabriel is a union grounded in mutual faith and a shared vision for God’s work. Together, they are blessed with a daughter, El-delight, who is the light of their lives. Priscillia's role as a mother is deeply intertwined with her spiritual mission, teaching her daughter the values of faith, love, and service.

Spiritual Journey and Inspirations

Priscillia's spiritual journey is marked by an unyielding dedication to living a life that pleases Jesus. Her daily walk is a testament to Philippians 1:21, "For to me, to live is Christ." This profound commitment is evident in her relentless intercession, her passionate mentorship, and her unwavering faith in God’s promises. Priscillia's life is a beacon of hope, demonstrating that a life devoted to God is both transformative and fulfilling.


Conclusion

Dr. Pastor Gabriel Priscillia’s story is one of remarkable grace, unwavering faith, and boundless love. Her academic achievements, spiritual fervor, and dedicated ministry make her a compelling figure in the body of Christ. She continues to inspire and uplift those around her, leading with a heart full of compassion and a spirit attuned to God's will.

Her legacy is one of intercession, mentorship, and steadfast devotion to Jesus. Through her life and ministry, Dr. Pastor Priscillia has profoundly impacted countless individuals, guiding them towards a deeper relationship with God and a life that glorifies Him. Her story is a testament to the incredible things God can do through a heart wholly surrendered to His purpose.
