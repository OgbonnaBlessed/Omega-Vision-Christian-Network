Experience the Power of God's Word: Sermons by Apostle Clement Gabriel

Welcome to the Sermons page of our church, where we invite you to delve deep into the rich and transformative teachings of our beloved man of God, Apostle Clement Gabriel. Each sermon is a beacon of light, designed to illuminate your path, deepen your relationship with God, and empower you to live a victorious Christian life.

Apostle Clement Gabriel, an anointed servant of God, delivers messages that are deeply rooted in the Word of God, inspired by the Holy Spirit, and aimed at equipping believers to walk in their God-given purpose. As it is written in Romans 10:17, "So then faith comes by hearing, and hearing by the word of God." Here, you will find sermons that will build your faith, encourage your spirit, and guide you in your walk with Christ.

Our sermons cover a wide range of topics, from spiritual growth and faith to healing, deliverance, and living a victorious life in Christ. They are not just messages but divine encounters designed to bring you closer to the heart of God.

Featured Sermon: "Walking in the Power of the Spirit"

In this powerful sermon, Apostle Clement Gabriel explores the depths of walking in the Spirit, drawing from Galatians 5:16, "So I say, walk by the Spirit, and you will not gratify the desires of the flesh." This message will empower you to live a life led by the Holy Spirit, overcoming the challenges of the flesh and experiencing the fullness of God's power in your daily life.

Transformative Teachings

Every sermon preached by Apostle Clement Gabriel is a divine appointment with God. These messages are crafted to address the needs of believers at different stages of their spiritual journey. As 2 Timothy 3:16-17 says, "All Scripture is God-breathed and is useful for teaching, rebuking, correcting and training in righteousness, so that the servant of God may be thoroughly equipped for every good work." Our goal is to ensure that through these sermons, you are thoroughly equipped for every good work.
